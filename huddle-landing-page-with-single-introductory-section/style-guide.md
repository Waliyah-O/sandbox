## Colors

### Primary

- Violet: hsl(257, 40%, 49%)

### Headings

- Family: [Poppins](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Poppins)

### Body

- Family: [Open Sans](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Open+Sans)
- Weights: 400
